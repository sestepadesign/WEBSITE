VERTICAL GARDEN

The Concept

Architectural Integration

Climbing and hanging plants predominated by green, red, and yellow tones. High capacity to withstand extreme temperatures

The meticulous selection of tropical plants, based on adaptation, structure, and colors, provided the space with a sensation of freshness, warm colors, and plants suspended in the air

The project proposal consists of two vertical gardens with different geometric structures, both indoors and outdoors, in combination with the space. Through avant-garde elements, the design aesthetics are focused on a vegetal lattice along the entire exterior facade: beams of light enter the interior space and achieve a jungle light effect. S’Estepa thanks this lovely project in collaboration with Vertical Garden Mallorca

– Reduction of space temperature by up to 40% on facades

– Sound: this garden has sound absorption and reduces space reverberation, improving sound quality

– Contributes to air purification: this garden traps 2.3kg of suspended dust per m2 over one year. It filters air particles and converts CO2 into O2, generating 1.7 kg of O2

– Generates well-being for employees and visitors

– Constitutes a philosophy towards nature

– Improves the company’s overall vision
