## Our Landscape Journey Design

### From Concept to Creation

We visit the place and immerse ourselves in its essence. We listen carefully to the property owner, considering the desired level of maintenance. Taking inspiration from the architectural context and the surrounding vegetation, we initiated a design concept. This concept evolves through the exploration of books, photographs, studies and our accumulated experiences, gradually shaping an imagined garden environment.

In addition, we prioritize the customer's contribution, understanding their desires, needs and expected investment in maintenance. Armed with this knowledge, we begin creating an atmosphere within our minds. This mindscape takes shape, refining itself to a state of perfection, ready to be translated onto paper.

In S'Estepa, it is a process where the site, the imagination and the client's collaboration harmonize to create exquisite outdoor spaces.

FIRST MEETING

Our first meeting, completely free of charge, will be to discuss your goals, preferences, and budget for your garden design project. We will also evaluate your site to determine the design, size, and existing features that may affect the design.

CONCEPTUAL DESIGN
Based on the information gathered during the initial consultation, we create a preliminary design concept that outlines the proposed layout, materials, and features for your garden. For this concept, we offer designs in 2D: Simple Design, 2D: Scale Design and/or 3D: Renders.

DESIGN REFINEMENT
We work closely with you to refine the design concept based on your feedback and preferences. We also make necessary adjustments to ensure that the design fits within your budget and practical needs.

FINAL DESIGN
Once the design concept has been finalized, we prepare detailed plans and specifications that outline the construction details and materials to be used. This includes plant selections, hardscape materials, and other features.

INSTALLATION
Our team of experienced builders brings your garden design to life, following the plans and specifications outlined in the final design. We manage the entire construction process, ensuring that the project is completed on time, within budget, and to your satisfaction.
