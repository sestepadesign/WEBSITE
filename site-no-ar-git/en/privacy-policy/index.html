Rights of the Data Subjects

S’ESTEPA JARDINERIA INTEGRAL SLU informs Users that they may exercise the rights of access, rectification, restriction, deletion, portability, objection to the processing of their personal data, and the right not to be subject to automated decision-making, including profiling, before the Data Controller, as well as the right to withdraw the given consent.

Your Rights

  • Right of Access: The right of the user to obtain confirmation of whether their data is being processed and, if so, the specific personal data being processed and the legal information about the processing.
  • Right of Rectification: The right of the affected party to have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected. Concerning the website, this only applies to information under the control of S'Estepa (such as removing comments, images, or personal details).
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: The right to limit the processing purposes originally intended by the Data Controller under certain circumstances.
  • Right of Deletion: The right to delete the user’s personal data, except as provided by the GDPR (freedom of expression, retention obligations, defense of claims).
  • Right to Portability: The right to receive the personal data provided in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format to transmit it to another controller.
  • Right of Objection: The user’s right to object to the processing of their personal data or to stop the processing when based on legitimate interest or direct marketing.
  • Right not to be subject to automated decision-making: The right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, producing legal effects.
  • Right to withdraw consent: For any processing based on consent, you have the right to withdraw it at any time, free of charge.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the data protection rights described above, you must follow these instructions:

Submit a written request to the address C/ FRA FRANCESC PALOU 6, BAJOS LOCAL 2, 07010 PALMA DE MALLORCA (ILLES BALEARS) (attention to S’ESTEPA JARDINERIA INTEGRAL SLU) or via email to administracio@sestepa.com.

The request sent by the personal data holder must consider the following:

  • It must be duly identified, and if there are doubts about the applicant’s identity, the request will be asked to be rectified (e.g., by requesting the DNI number, a copy of the DNI, the email provided, etc.).
  • The request can be made by a legal or voluntary representative when they are duly identified and authorized by the data holder (through an express authorization of the data holder to exercise the personal rights regulated in the personal data protection regulations).
  • The request must specify the rights being exercised. If it does not refer to a specific treatment, the general information will be provided.
